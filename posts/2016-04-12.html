Exercise 1.3. Define a procedure that takes three numbers as arguments and returns the sum of the
squares of the two larger numbers.


CL-USER> (defun sum-two-larger-in-three-numbers(x y z)
	   (cond ( (and (<= x y) (<= x z)) (+ y z) )
		 ( (and (<= y x) (<= y z)) (+ x z) )
		 ( (and (<= z x) (<= z y)) (+ x y) )))
SUM-TWO-LARGER-IN-THREE-NUMBERS
CL-USER> (sum-two-larger-in-three-numbers 1 3 4)
7
CL-USER> (sum-two-larger-in-three-numbers 1 -3 4)
5
CL-USER> (sum-two-larger-in-three-numbers 1 -3 -4)
-2
CL-USER> (sum-two-larger-in-three-numbers 11 3 -4)
14
